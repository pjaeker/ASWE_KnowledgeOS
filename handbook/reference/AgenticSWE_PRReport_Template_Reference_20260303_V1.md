> Fail-closed: Wenn Pflichtfelder fehlen, Writer/Review behandelt es als **fail**.

## Summary (What)

- Änderungen (1–3 Bulletpoints)
- Betroffene Pfade/Dateien

## Motivation (Why)

- Warum?
- Alternative verworfen?

## Scope & Constraints

- Allowlist eingehalten? ja/nein
- Blocklist/Stop-&-Ask berührt? nein/ja (welche)

## Risk Assessment

- Risikostufe: low / medium / high
- Risiken (max 3)
- Mitigation (je Risiko 1 Zeile)

## Gates & Checks (Evidence)

- Write-Gates: pass/fail + Hinweis
- CI Required Checks:
  - Lint: pass/fail (Run-ID/Link)
  - Links: pass/fail (Run-ID/Link)
  - Taxonomy↔Glossary: pass/fail (Run-ID/Link)
  - Frontmatter/Tags: pass/fail (Run-ID/Link)
- Security (wenn aktiv): Tool + Report-ID + pass/fail
- Secrets Scan: pass/fail

## Rollback Plan

- Schritte (1–2)
- Rollback-Risiko: low/medium/high

## Notes

- Agent beteiligt? nein/ja (Copilot/Codex/Chat)
- Follow-ups (optional)
