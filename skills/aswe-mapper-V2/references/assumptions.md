## Assumptions

This skill is a bounded mapper role optimized for GPT-Pro as main agent.

- The mapper inherits the effective session contract before suggesting files or slices.
- In package-local refactor work, `context/effective-latest/` outranks older repo-github-sync extracts.
- Repo-local `.codex` implementations may specialize the mapper, but they must not weaken anchor-first routing.
- The mapper does not replace verify, runtime diagnosis, or documentation wording roles.
