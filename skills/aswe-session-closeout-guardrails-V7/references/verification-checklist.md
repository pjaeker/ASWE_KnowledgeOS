# Verification checklist

## Fresh-evidence preflight

Before artifact decisions, check:
- fresher entry exists?
- fresher status exists?
- fresher workstream exists?
- fresher structural docs exist?
- fresher protocols or codex-app guidance exist?
- fresher commits materially changed routing or operating guidance?

## Artifact decision readiness

Do not decide `no-change` unless you checked the relevant current anchor.
