## Assumptions

This skill is a bounded triage role optimized for GPT-Pro as main agent.

- The triager inherits the effective session contract before reordering hypotheses.
- Repo-local `.codex` implementations may specialize the role, but materialization is currently optional-later rather than guaranteed.
- The triager does not replace fresh observation, scope mapping, or verify planning when those remain the true missing step.
- In package-local refactor work, the triager is especially useful for reconciling competing interpretations of package and context files.
